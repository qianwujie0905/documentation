
Smart Fill lets you **automatically fill empty areas** with AI-generated content that matches your page’s style — including **colors, spacing, layout, and visual tone**.

It’s the fastest way to make unfinished sections look complete and consistent.

---

## 1. Click “Smart Fill”

Open the plugin and select **Smart Fill**.

<img 
  src="/images/smart fill/button.png"
  alt="Smart Fill button"
  width="520"
/>

Describe what you want AI to add to the selected empty area.

For this example, we use:

> **“Add a simple background shape or light pattern to make the experience part more visual.”**

---

## 2. AI Generates a Visual Design That Fits Your Style

Smart Fill analyzes your page’s typography, spacing, and color system to generate a **smooth, style-matching visual pattern**.

It keeps everything aligned with your existing design.

<img 
  src="/images/smart fill/result.png"
  alt="Smart Fill result"
  width="520"
/>

The empty “Experience” section now becomes a polished and visually enhanced part of the page — created in seconds.

---

## When to Use Smart Fill

- When a section feels empty or visually flat  
- When you want subtle background shapes  
- When you need placeholder visuals that match your design  
- When you want AI to finish a layout without breaking consistency  

Smart Fill is perfect for **resume pages, landing pages, dashboards, and product UI layouts**.
