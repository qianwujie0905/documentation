# Welcome to Prompt.to.design

[Prompt.to.design](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1528325292196938261) is an AI-powered assistant for Figma that helps designers create, modify, and optimize user interface layouts using natural language.  
It understands your design language, adapts to your existing style system, and produces consistent, editable results directly within Figma.

![Main Overview](/images/main.png)

## Overview

The plugin simplifies the design process by automating layout generation, style adaptation, and content filling.  
Whether you are building a landing page, dashboard, or component library, Prompt.to.design helps you move from idea to visual output in seconds.

## Key Features

- Generate layouts from text descriptions  
- Learn and apply design styles from selected frames  
- Modify specific areas or elements intelligently  
- Seamlessly fill blank spaces with AI-generated content  
- Maintain full control and consistency within Figma  

## Who It is For

Prompt.to.design is designed for individual designers, teams, and creative professionals who want to accelerate the design process while keeping precision and style alignment.
