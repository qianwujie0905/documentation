# Welcome to Prompt.to.design

**Prompt.to.design** is an AI-powered assistant for Figma that helps designers create, edit, and prototype layouts - **10 times faster with AI**.

It understands your style, adapts to your existing design system, and turns your ideas into polished, editable Figma layouts instantly.  
Whether you're a beginner or a pro, Prompt.to.design helps you design like a professional in seconds.

<img src="/images/main.png" alt="Prompt.to.design banner" />

## Overview

Prompt.to.design streamlines your workflow by generating layouts, transferring styles, redesigning sections, and filling empty areas intelligently - all inside Figma.

From landing pages to dashboards, marketing sites, or mobile UI, it helps you move from **idea to visual output** in seconds.

## Key Features

- **Kickstart new designs from a prompt**  
  Describe what you want (e.g., *"a modern dashboard with sidebar and stats card"*), and get a clean, editable result that matches your Figma style.

- **Style Transfer between frames**  
  Pick any frame as a visual reference and apply its color, typography, and spacing to a brand-new layout.

- **Region Redesign**  
  Highlight part of a frame, and Prompt.to.design will update just that section while keeping the rest consistent.

- **Smart Fill**  
  Fill blank areas with AI-generated content and structure that fits your existing layout.

- **Stay 100% in Figma**  
  All outputs are fully editable native Figma layers - no switching tools or exporting.

## Who It Is For

Prompt.to.design is perfect for:

- Designers exploring variations quickly  
- Figma beginners learning by AI generation  
- Product teams prototyping fast  
- Anyone turning ideas into visuals instantly  
- Creatives who want to stay consistent with brand/style while designing faster
