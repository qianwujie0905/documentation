## Getting Started
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Prompt.to.design?">
    Prompt.to.design is an AI-powered assistant for Figma that helps designers create and refine layouts using natural language. It understands your intent, learns style references, and produces editable Figma frames that fit your design system.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What can I build with Prompt.to.design?">
    You can generate landing pages, dashboards, product interfaces, and marketing layouts. Results are component-based, Auto Layout-ready, and compatible with your libraries.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need design experience to use it?">
    No. Describe the layout you want and the plugin generates a professional, editable design. You can then fine-tune using Region Design or Modify Elements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get started?">
    Install the plugin from the Figma Community, launch it in your file, and click <em>Kick Start</em>. Enter a short description, optionally upload references, then generate your first layout.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Features and Usage
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are the core features?">
    Kick Start, Style Transfer, Region Design, Smart Fill, and Modify Elements. They cover creation, consistency, partial redesign, content completion, and fine-tuning.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does it work with Figma components?">
    Yes. Generated layouts use frames, Auto Layout, and components, keeping your libraries and tokens intact.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upload my own references?">
    Yes. Upload images, PDFs, or screenshots. The model analyzes visual cues and adapts the resulting layout and style.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many generations can I run?">
    Free Trial allows up to 20 generations per day. Plus Plan provides 100 monthly credits and advanced integration.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account and Billing
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does credit usage work?">
    A generation or major modification consumes one credit. Credits reset monthly for paid plans. Daily limits apply in Free Trial.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or cancel my plan?">
    Yes. Manage your plan from the billing page. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data stored securely?">
    Yes. Uploaded assets and metadata are processed under strict privacy standards and are not shared across accounts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
